CFDconsult

  • Structural analysis of buildings under fire conditions> : Engineering analysis of building structures exposed to fire, including thermal loading, material degradation (wood), large deformations untill destruction. This includes justification towards fire autority according on the local rules. All the studies are based on the following software : SAFIR (structural analysis), Fire dynamics simulation (LES simulation), Ozone (fire simulation).
  • Fire and risk analysis : Quantitative and qualitative fire risk analysis combining fire scenarios, heat release rates, smoke propagation, and human and structural consequences. This expertise supports safety studies, design optimization, compliance with regulations, and decision-making under uncertainty for safety-critical infrastructures.
  • Air Traffic Control (ATC) speech recognition solutions : Development of speech recognition systems tailored to Air Traffic Control (ATC) environments, accounting for constrained vocabulary, operational phraseology, background noise, and safety requirements. This tool aims to help pilots and then drone with ATC transcription.
  • Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) : Numerical simulation of fluid flows, heat transfer, and turbulence for engineering applications. The simulations include RANS or LES.
